FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website for my business?

Most local business websites are live within 2–3 weeks from the date we receive your content and branding assets. Rush timelines are available if needed.

Will my website show up on Google?

Yes. Every website we build includes foundational on-page SEO — proper page titles, meta descriptions, schema markup, Google Maps integration, and site speed optimization. We also offer full local SEO packages for businesses that want to rank aggressively.

I'm not tech-savvy. Will I be able to manage the website myself?

Absolutely. We build on platforms that are easy to manage. We also provide a short walkthrough video after launch so you know how to make basic updates. And we're always just a message away.

Do you only work with businesses in India?

No — we work with local service businesses globally. Most of our clients are in the US, UK, Canada, and Australia, though we welcome businesses from anywhere.

What makes you different from other web design agencies?

We specialize exclusively in local service businesses. We're not a generalist agency trying to serve everyone. We understand the specific challenges of HVAC companies, plumbers, pet salons, and similar businesses — and we build websites and marketing systems specifically designed for your type of customer.

How much does a website cost?

Our local business websites start at $1,200. The exact investment depends on your specific needs. Book a free call and we'll give you a clear quote within 24 hours.

Do you offer ongoing support after the website is launched?

Yes. All plans include post-launch support. Our Growth and Full Dominance packages include ongoing monthly services including SEO, ads management, and reporting.

How do I get started?

Simply book a free 20-minute strategy call. We'll learn about your business, answer all your questions, and tell you exactly what we'd recommend — with no obligation to sign up.
