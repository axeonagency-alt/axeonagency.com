QUESTIONS?

We’ve got answers. Or at least some very educated guesses. Let’s demystify the digital wizardry.

01.

What exactly do you guys do? Are you designers, developers, or just wizards?

A bit of all three. We combine mind-bending design with rock-solid engineering. We build websites that don’t just look like art—they work like high-performance machines.

No templates, no boring layouts, and definitely no magic wands. Just a lot of caffeine, deep research, and clean code.

02.

What on earth is AEO? Is it SEO’s cooler younger sibling?

Precisely. AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) is all about making sure AI engines (like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Perplexity) actually know who you are and recommend your brand when users ask them questions.

If SEO is about getting found on Google, AEO is about being the primary recommendation when an AI does the searching for you. We optimize your semantic data so AI models cite you as the authority.

03.

Why shouldn't I just build my site with a $50 template?

You absolutely can—if you want to look exactly like three hundred other businesses and have a site that loads slower than dial-up. Templates are bloated, generic, and notoriously hard to scale.

We craft bespoke digital ecosystems designed around your exact business goals. Our sites load instantly, rank higher, and convert passive visitors into absolute brand advocates.

04.

How long does a project take? Can we launch tomorrow?

Unless "tomorrow" is in a parallel universe where time moves ten times faster, probably not! A premium, custom-coded brand experience typically takes between 6 to 12 weeks from strategy to launch.

We break this down into collaborative phases: Discovery, Wireframing & UX, Visual Design, Development, and rigorous QA testing. Quality takes a minute, but it lasts years.

05.

Will my website work on mobile? My uncle says everyone uses phones now.

Your uncle is incredibly wise. Over 60% of all web traffic is mobile, which is why we build mobile-first. We design layouts that adapt seamlessly from giant 8K desktop displays to compact smartphones.

We test every interaction, button size, and load speed on actual physical devices to ensure the mobile experience is just as fluid and breathtaking as the desktop version.

06.

Do you guys do SEO, or do you just make things look pretty?

Looking pretty is just the cover letter. A beautiful website that no one can find is a digital tragedy. That's why SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is baked into our development DNA.

We structure semantic HTML, optimize assets to load in milliseconds, write clean schema markups, and build logical site architectures. We build sites that search engines fall in love with.

07.

Can we edit the site ourselves, or do we need to hire you forever?

No hostage situations here! We build on intuitive Content Management Systems (CMS) or create bespoke headless dashboards tailored to your workflow.

You’ll be able to easily swap images, edit copy, write blog posts, and add new case studies without needing to write a single line of code. We also provide personalized video walkthroughs at handoff to make you a pro.

08.

What is your branding process? How do you define a brand's soul?

We start by digging deep. We run discovery workshops to uncover your brand's core values, target audience, and positioning. We don't just design a logo—we build a comprehensive visual language.

This includes typography, color psychology, custom iconography, voice & tone guidelines, and layout systems that ensure your brand looks cohesive across all physical and digital touchpoints.
